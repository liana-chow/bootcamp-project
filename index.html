Bootcamp 2020: Flower Garden

My first programming project, concluding the 2 week Programming Bootcamp at City (2020). This Processing project demonstrates a comprehensive understanding of the use of arrays, loops, and randomness. I created a flower garden scene where the amount of flowers, their starting height and final height are randomized. The player would interact with it by "pushing" the ends of the stem to grow. Finally, once all of the flowers have grown, an ending message is displayed.

Check out the code on Github: https://liana-chow.github.io/
